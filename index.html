What is a Forex EA?

A Forex EA is an automated trading program that runs on MetaTrader 4 (MT4) or MetaTrader 5 (MT5) platforms. It is designed to open, manage, and close trades according to predefined rules and algorithms—without requiring constant manual input. With the right EA, you can:

  • Backtest strategies before going live
